HTML Project: Exploring London

Project 1 Consists in creating an informative website. The information of the website is about a place (city, country, or state) where you want to inform people about that place. In your website you should have to present four pieces information about the place such as:

  • Why London is a famous place
  • Activites to do in London
  • Important details and locations in London
  • Photos of London
